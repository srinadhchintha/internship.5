About Amazon

Welcome to Amazon — Earth’s most customer-centric company. We deliver everything you need, fast and reliably.

Our Journey

Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, Amazon started as an online bookstore and quickly evolved into the largest online retailer in the world. We now serve millions of customers with products ranging from books and electronics to fashion and cloud services.

Our Mission

To be Earth’s most customer-centric company where customers can find and discover anything they might want to buy online at the lowest possible prices.

What We Offer

  • Millions of products across all major categories
  • Fast, reliable delivery through Amazon Prime
  • Secure checkout and order tracking
  • 24/7 customer service and support
  • Easy returns and product guarantees

Core Values

Amazon values innovation, operational excellence, and long-term thinking. We continuously invest in technology and infrastructure to serve customers better every day.

Our Global Team

Behind Amazon is a global team of developers, warehouse workers, customer service agents, and technologists all united by one mission: to make shopping seamless and delightful for everyone.
